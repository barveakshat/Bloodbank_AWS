BLOOD BANK MANAGEMENT SYETEM

WITH AWS INTEGRATION

A comprehensive Blood Bank Management System integrated with AWS that enhances blood resource management, user experience, and healthcare services delivery.

PROPOSED SYSTEM

The proposed system allows users to add details of their own from anywhere in India and respective doctors can get the details of the donor for further process. This system helps doctors and blood inventory management staff in organizing and supplying the blood needed.

Database and Information Management

Develop a centralized database to track blood product inventory, including donor information, and product availability. Implement a user-friendly interface for healthcare staff to access and update patient records, transfusion orders, and blood product requests.

User Support and Maintenance:

Provide technical support for users and ensure the system's ongoing maintenance and updates.
